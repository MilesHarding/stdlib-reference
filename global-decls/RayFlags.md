## Availability and Requirements

Defined for the following targets:

#### hlsl
Available in stages: `closesthit`, `anyhit`, `miss`, `intersection`.

#### glsl
Available in stages: `closesthit`, `anyhit`, `miss`, `intersection`.

#### cuda
Available in stages: `closesthit`, `anyhit`, `miss`, `intersection`.

#### spirv
Available in stages: `closesthit`, `anyhit`, `miss`, `intersection`.

Requires capability: `spvRayTracingKHR`.
